What is Customer Service ?

Customer service refers to the support and assistance provided to customers before, during, and after purchasing a product or service. It encompasses a range of activities aimed at enhancing customer satisfaction and building loyalty. Key aspects of customer service include:

  • Assistance: Helping customers with inquiries, issues, or product information.
  • Support: Providing technical help or troubleshooting for products and services.
  • Communication: Engaging with customers through various channels, such as phone, email, chat, and social media.
  • Problem Resolution: Addressing and resolving complaints or concerns effectively.
  • Feedback: Gathering customer feedback to improve products and services.
